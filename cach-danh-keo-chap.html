Xem Chi Tiet Bai Viet Tai: Cach Danh Keo Chap

1. Hieu ro ve keo chap

De co the choi keo chap thanh cong, dieu dau tien ban can lam la hieu ro ve loai keo nay. Keo chap co nghia la doi bong yeu hon se duoc cong them mot so diem truoc khi bat dau tran dau. Vi vay, de thang keo chap, doi bong yeu hon phai gianh duoc ket qua tot hon doi bong manh hon sau khi cong diem chap.

Vi du, neu tran dau giua Manchester United va Fulham co keo chap la Man Utd -1.5, dieu do co nghia la Man Utd se phai gianh chien thang voi cach biet 2 ban tro len de thang keo chap. Neu Man Utd thang voi cach biet 1 ban, keo se duoc tinh la hoa va ban se khong thang hay thua gi.



2. Xem xet cac yeu to anh huong den tran dau

Truoc khi danh keo chap, ban can xem xet ky cac yeu to anh huong den tran dau nhu phong do cua hai doi, su vang mat cua cau thu chu chot, thay doi quan trong trong doi hinh, san nha hay san khach, thoi tiet, lich su doi dau giua hai doi, v.v...

Nhung yeu to nay se anh huong den ket qua cua tran dau va giup ban dua ra quyet dinh chinh xac khi choi keo chap.

3. Chon doi bong phu hop

Khi danh keo chap, ban can chon doi bong ma ban tin tuong se thang tran dau. Dieu nay co the dua tren su phan tich cua ban ve cac yeu to anh huong den tran dau hoac dua tren nhan dinh cua cac chuyen gia.

Neu ban khong co du thong tin de dua ra quyet dinh, hay dat cuoc vao doi bong ma ban yeu thich. Tuy nhien, can luu y rang khong nen dat cuoc qua nhieu vao so thich ca nhan ma bo qua cac yeu to quan trong khac.

4. Dung cac meo hay

De tang co hoi thang keo chap, ban co the ap dung mot so meo hay nhu:

  • Chon nhung tran dau co ty le keo chap thap hon 0.5 hoac 1.0 vi ty le thang se cao hon.
  • Chon cac doi bong co phong do tot va dang co mach thang tai thoi diem hien tai.
  • Chon cac doi bong duoc danh gia co co hoi thang cao hon doi bong khac.
  • Dat cuoc vao thoi gian cuoi cung truoc khi tran dau bat dau de co the cap nhat duoc thong tin moi nhat.
  • Choi voi so tien cuoc nho va khong choi qua nhieu tran cung mot luc de tranh rui ro.

5. Tim hieu ve nha cai

Trong linh vuc ca cuoc online, viec chon nha cai uy tin va dang tin cay la rat quan trong. Ban nen tim hieu ky ve nha cai truoc khi dat cuoc de dam bao an toan cho minh.

Nhung nha cai lon va co tieng se cung cap cho ban nhung ty le keo chap hop ly va co luat choi ro rang. Dong thoi, ho cung co chinh sach bao mat va ho tro khach hang tot, giup ban co the tham gia ca cuoc mot cach an toan va tien loi.

6. Kien nhan va binh tinh

Cuoi cung, de thanh cong trong viec danh keo chap, ban can kien nhan va binh tinh. Danh keo chap cung co the gap that bai va ban khong nen lo lang hay nan chi khi do xay ra. Dieu quan trong la hay tim hieu va rut kinh nghiem de cai thien ky nang choi keo chap cua minh.

Ban cung nen dat muc tieu thang nho va kien tri theo do. Dung qua tham lam va dat muc tieu qua cao, dieu do se khien ban mat kien nhan va that bai.
